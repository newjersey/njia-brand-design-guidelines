The New Jersey Innovation Authority brand should make public-sector innovation feel practical,
credible, and accessible. The guidance in this section defines the standards behind individual
design choices.

## Brand Promise

NJIA helps public institutions design and deliver better services for New Jerseyans. Brand
expression should make that work feel clear, useful, and connected to measurable public value.

## Design Principles

- **Clear before clever.** Prefer direct composition, plain language, and recognizable hierarchy.
- **Institutional, not distant.** The brand should feel trustworthy without becoming cold or generic.
- **Practical by default.** Materials should make it easy for a reader to act, decide, or learn.
- **Accessible from the start.** Visual and written communication must work across ability,
  device, language familiarity, and context.

## Governance

Document every approved rule in this site. When a guideline changes, update the related page and
include enough context for future contributors to understand why the decision changed.
